Connect

COMMMONS Connect is a CRM interface for staying in contact with patients, and assisting with speeding up patient relations
  • Highly customizable mass emails/texts (inclement weather closures, newsletters, new treatments/services, etc)
  • Appointment reminders/confirmation/reschedules
  • Follow ups for re-evaluation / re-treatment
  • Remind patients of account balances or upcoming payment plan charges
  • Keep an accurate call/contact log for each patient
Combined with COMMMONS Care, we also offer a flexible patient interface into your EHR system, called COMMMONS.me
